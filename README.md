# NETFLIX-MOVIES-ANALYSIS-USING-PYTON
Netflix movie analysis : ratings , directors , release year


This analysis explores Netflix releases and director trends using Python. 

Key insights are summarized below:

**CONTENT OVERVIEW**

Netflix hosts nearly three times more movies than TV shows.

Top Ratings:

TV-MA (most common)

TV-14

TV-PG

Top Content Categories:

International Movies

Dramas

Independent Movies

TV Dramas

Romantic Movies

**RELEASE TRENDS**

Monthly Releases:

Highest in July, followed by December, September, and April.

Yearly Releases:

2018: 1,147 releases (767 movies, 380 TV shows) – peak year

2017: 1,032 releases (767 movies, 265 TV shows)

2019: 1,030 releases (633 movies, 397 TV shows)

**COUNTRY LEVEL RELEASES**

United States: 3,211 releases

India: 1,008 releases

United Kingdom: 628 releases

**DIRECTOR INSIGHTS**

Most directors created only one movie or TV show.

Several directors made 2–3 movies, and a few produced 9–19 movies.

For TV shows:

215 directors made 1 show

6 directors made 2 shows

1 director made 3 shows

**LIBRARIES**

Numpy , Pandas, Matplotlib
